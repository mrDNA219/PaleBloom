Game Concept:
    We (the player) are scientists who are tasked with raising genetically engineered creature companions that we will take on missions to parallel dimension versions of Earth to discover alternate sources of renewable energy. 
    The creatures will rapidly evolve in the parallel dimensions and we will witness, record, and study their growth. Each "world" the player visits with his/her creature will contain various puzzles that must be completed in a given time limit. The puzzles will be thematically structured 
    around the emergent adaptations of the creature to the alien environment and the player's task of maintaining the health of the creature using various tools (which they will unlock more of/improve over time). 

Art Style:
    Scavenger's Reign, a Netflix sci-fi animated series.
    Synergy, an indie environmental strategy/simulation Steam game.
