Game Concept:
    We (the player) are scientists who are tasked with raising genetically engineered creature companions that we will take on missions to parallel dimension versions of Earth to discover alternate sources of renewable and clean energy. 
    The creatures will rapidly evolve in the parallel dimensions and we will witness and record their evolutions for clues about the nature of energy and adaptation in the universe. Furthermore, along the way we will (hopefully) rediscover our 
    bond with the natural world and find not only a way to heal our planet but ourselves spiritually. 

Art Style:
    Synergy, an indie environmental strategy/simulation Steam game. 
    Scavenger's Reign, a Netflix sci-fi animated series
